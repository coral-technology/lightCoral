The one platform for healthcare- Why did we try to solve a broken

Our mission is simple: We think health plans should know the price of medical services up front, and providers should get paid extremely fast, without having to fight for what they’ve done. So we’ve built a system to simplify that process, and make it easy for everyone.

Coral was founded in 2016 by successful serial entrepreneur Gregory Smith. Greg was new to healthcare in 2016, but had a 30 year track record of founding successful companies. All four of his previous companies operated on the same principle- taking extremely complex problems and making them simple, and solvable for everyone.
